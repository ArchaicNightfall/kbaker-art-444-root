Frequently Asked Questions

When will you be graduating from SDSU?

    I'm currently in my final semester of SDSU. I will be graduating in the next upcoming months in May of 2025.

What do you have planned after graduation?

    Once I have my Bachelors in Art Multimedia and away from academia. I will be searching for a job with decent pay. I also want to complete a few personal art and writing projects.

What program do you prefer to use?

    Depends on what I'm working with. If I want to do photomanipulations and editing, I prefer to use Photoshop. If I'm working on animation or digital comics, I prefer to use Clip Studio Paint.

What work are you most proud of? Why?

    I used to be terrible with backgrounds, using gradients or characters in a white void. My current favorite has a red and gray color palette of an artifical zoo, with the foreground having two of my original characters facing off. It's not in my gallery since it's not a commission.
